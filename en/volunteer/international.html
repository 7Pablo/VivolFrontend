International Volunteering

Join international volunteering and bring your support to communities in various countries, all while immersing yourself in unique and diverse cultures. Live unforgettable adventures, connect with people from around the world, and leave a positive impact wherever you go. Your participation can transform lives!

ICYE Volunteering Program

The International Cultural Youth Exchange (ICYE) federation is a non-profit organization with over 65 years of experience promoting global citizenship. Since its beginnings, ICYE has encouraged youth mobility through cultural exchanges and volunteering around the world. Its main goal is to eliminate prejudice and foster intercultural understanding, building a fairer and more peaceful world together.

ICYE organizes short- and long-term exchange programs in over 40 countries, where you combine your stay with volunteer service. It offers participants the opportunity to immerse themselves in another culture while doing social work abroad, gaining access to multicultural education and non-formal learning.

At VIVOL, we are part of the ICYE network, promoting these youth exchanges both short-term (1 to 5 months) and long-term (6 months to 1 year). Through this international volunteering program, we want more young people to understand their role as members of a global society, fostering understanding between different cultures and nations.

Program Benefits

The ICYE program is designed to guide the volunteer throughout their immersion experience in another country, its culture, and their service project. During your international volunteering, you will have the following benefits:

Tangible Benefits

  • Accommodation: free lodging with a host family or project.
  • Meals: 3 daily meals adapted to the local cuisine.
  • International insurance: medical coverage, life insurance, and liability insurance during your stay.
  • Local transportation: basic mobility covered (depending on the project/country).
  • Pre-departure training: seminars and preparation activities in Peru before departure.
  • On-site orientation: follow-up and orientation activities once you are in your host country.
  • ICYE certificate: official participation certificate upon completion of the program.
  • Basic language course: introductory lessons in the local language (for long-term programs).
  • Pocket money: a monthly financial allowance for personal expenses (for long-term programs).

Intangible Benefits

  • Professional growth: you will gain international experience that any employer will value.
  • Language learning: you will practice and improve in another language naturally.
  • Cultural openness: you will learn about new cultures and realities, broadening your view of the world.
  • Global network of friends: you will make international contacts and friendships for life.
  • Personal growth: you will gain independence, confidence, and maturity.
  • Greater social empathy: you will develop sensitivity to social issues and how to address them.
  • Unforgettable adventures!: you will experience travels, fun moments, and challenges that will positively impact you.

The program doesn't include

  • Airfare
  • Visa costs for the destination country
  • Departure or entry taxes

ICYE Program Application Process

What do you need to participate in the ICYE program?

Age: Be between 18 and 35 years old (the exact limit depends on the destination country).

Service vocation: Willingness to contribute by doing social work in the community.

Adaptability: Motivation to live in another country and immerse yourself in a different culture.

Preparation: Availability to attend all pre-departure preparation activities organized by VIVOL.

Financing: Ability to cover the cost of participation in the ICYE program.

Travel: Means to pay for your airfare and necessary visa procedures.
